Create a comprehensive Support Center page for SBEs (Small Business Enterprises). The page should provide clear guidance on how to navigate the CaltransBizConnect platform, find contracting opportunities, and contact help desks.

**Page Structure:**
1. Global Header with navigation.
2. Search bar for FAQ/Support articles.
3. Category cards (e.g., "Registration Help," "Finding Opportunities," "System Requirements").
4. "Contact an Expert" section with a form or contact details.
5. Footer with helpful links.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Light, Clean, Professional
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Primary Accent: Caltrans Blue (#005A8C)
- Secondary Accent: Caltrans Orange (#F7941D)
- Text Primary: Black (#000000)
- Font: Arial, Calibri
- Layout: Structured, Grid-based, Accessible
