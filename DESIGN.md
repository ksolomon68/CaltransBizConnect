# Design System: CaltransBizConnect
**Project ID:** 8488858525033049831

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Professional, clean, and highly accessible, following Caltrans editorial standards. The aesthetic philosophy prioritizes clarity and high contrast (WCAG 2.1 AA compliant).

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Caltrans Blue** (#005A8C) – Primary brand color, headers, primary buttons.
- **Caltrans Orange** (#F7941D) – Accent color, secondary buttons, highlights.
- **Standard Black** (#000000) – Body text, high-contrast readability.
- **White** (#FFFFFF) – Primary background.
- **Silver** (#CCCCCC) – Borders and neutral backgrounds.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Sans-Serif (Primary):** Arial, Calibri, sans-serif.
- **Standard Size:** 11-14pt (16px base).
- **Headings:** Bold, letter-spacing -0.02em.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** 2px solid border, 8px border-radius, medium weight text.
* **Cards:** White background, light silver border, 12px border-radius, subtle shadow.
* **Inputs:** 1px border, 4px border-radius, high-contrast focus states (Blue shadow).

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Grid:** 1280px max-width container.
- **White Space:** Generous padding (1.5rem / 24px) for readability.
- **Clarity:** Simple, direct layouts with clear hierarchical headings.
